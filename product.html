Product Description

The Sony A7 III combines high-performance imaging with professional-grade video capabilities in a compact mirrorless body. Its 24.2MP back-illuminated full-frame sensor delivers exceptional image quality with remarkable clarity and low noise, even in challenging lighting conditions.

Key Features:

  • 24.2MP Full-frame Exmor R CMOS sensor
  • BIONZ X image processor
  • 4K HDR video recording
  • 5-axis in-body image stabilization
  • 693-point phase-detection AF system
  • 10fps continuous shooting with AF/AE tracking
  • High-contrast 2.36M-dot OLED viewfinder
  • 3.0" 922k-dot tilting LCD touchscreen
  • Dual SD card slots (UHS-II compatible)
  • Weather-sealed magnesium alloy body

Perfect for:

Whether you're shooting portraits, landscapes, or fast-moving subjects, the A7 III's advanced autofocus system and high-speed continuous shooting ensure you never miss a moment. Its exceptional low-light performance makes it ideal for wedding and event photography, while 4K video capabilities cater to videographers and content creators.
